Pulsar 200NS

Bajaj Pulsar NS200 was launched for the very first time back in 2012. The launch set the tone for the next generations of Pulsar and what they might look like. After initial launch, the bike went AWOL and made a comeback in early 2017. The later versions went through some cosmetic changes and a re-tuned engine that was compliant with the latest norms. The bike is placed alongside Pulsar AS200 and Pulsar 220F.
